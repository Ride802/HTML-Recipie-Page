Recipe #1 - Goatmeal

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Oatmeal 1.5 cups
Coconut Milk 2 cups
Maple Syrup 1/4 cup

Step 1: Pour oatmeal into bowl
Step 2: Pour coconut milk into bowl
Step 3: Pour maple syrup into bowl

Recipe #2 - Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 6 eggs
Coconut Oil 2 tablespoons
Salt 2 teaspoons

Step 1: Melt coconut oil in pan on medium heat
Step 2: Crack eggs into pan. Only use one hand to crack eggs
Step 3: Mix eggs every 2.5 minutes
Step 4: Remove eggs from pan and add salt

Source: Mateo's Creations

Recipe #3 - Protein Shake

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Protein Powder 4 scoops
Coconut Milk 3 cups
Peanut Butter 4 teaspoons

Step 1: Pour coconut milk into blender
Step 2:Pour protein powder into blender
Step 3:Scoop peanut butter into blender
Step 4:Blend on high for 30 seconds
